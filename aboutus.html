🐾 Welcome to KittyPups Clinic

Where Paws Are Family!

At KittyPups Clinic, we believe pets are more than companions—they're family. That’s why we’ve created a cozy, caring clinic where every paw is treated with love, patience, and professional care.

Nestled in the heart of the city, our clinic welcomes dogs, cats, rabbits, and birds with open arms and gentle hands. We’re pet parents too, so we understand how much your furry friend means to you. From soft blankets on our tables to treats in every pocket, KittyPups is a place your pet can feel at home.

  • 💉 Vaccinations & Preventive Care
  • 🐛 Deworming & Parasite Control
  • 🧪 Blood Tests & Lab Diagnostics

  • ✂ Grooming, Nail Trimming & Basic Hygiene

👩‍⚕ About Our Vets

Our vets are gentle, patient, and truly love what they do. With years of experience and a calm touch, they’re trusted by pets and loved by pet parents.

We know some pets can be a little shy or anxious—that’s okay. Our doctors take their time, speak softly, and treat every animal like their own.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend calling ahead for smooth service.

What animals do you treat?
We happily care for dogs, cats, rabbits, and birds.

Do you offer grooming?
Yes! We provide basic grooming, nail trimming, and hygiene care.

What should I bring to my pet’s first visit?
Bring any medical records, vaccination history if available, and of course—your pet!
